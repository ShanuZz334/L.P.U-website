Our Achivements

NAAC A++

Lovely Professional University has achieved the highest grade of A++ from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), scoring an impressive 3.68 out of 4. This top-tier ranking reflects LPU's excellence in teaching, research, and infrastructure. The NAAC A++ grade signifies LPU's commitment to maintaining high standards in higher education, offering quality resources and support for its students. This recognition places LPU among India's elite institutions, demonstrating its dedication to providing an outstanding learning environment.

NBA Accreditation

Lovely Professional University is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), which assesses the qualitative competence of programs offered by educational institutions from diploma level to postgraduate level in engineering, technology, management, pharmacy, architecture, and related disciplines. NBA accreditation ensures that LPU's programs meet high standards of quality and relevance, preparing students for industry demands and enhancing their employability.

WURI RANKING

Lovely Professional University has achieved a remarkable feat by securing the 4th position among Indian universities and 126th rank globally in the World University Rankings for Innovation (WURI) 2024 This prestigious ranking highlights LPU's commitment to fostering innovation and creativity among its students and faculty. LPU's innovative approach and emphasis on the Fourth Industrial Revolution have placed it ahead of many renowned global institutions

More About Us

Lovely Professional University (LPU) has a fascinating history of growth and innovation since its inception in 2005 by the Lovely International Trust. Located in Phagwara, Punjab, LPU quickly became one of India's largest and most influential private universities. Over the years, it has expanded its offerings to include a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. LPU is ranked 36th among the top Indian universities in the Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2022 and has received the highest NAAC Grade of A++ with a score of 3.68 on a scale of 4. The university continues to evolve, embracing new technologies and methodologies to stay at the forefront of academic excellence
