About Our Recruters

Lovely Professional University boasts a robust placement cell that collaborates with a wide range of top recruiters Renowned companies such as Amazon, Cognizant, Infosys, Adobe, and Microsoft regularly participate in LPU's placement drives The university's strong industry connections and proactive placement cell ensure that students have ample opportunities to secure rewarding careers. LPU's placement record is impressive, with many students receiving packages of INR 10 LPA and above The highest package offered in recent years has been around INR 54.75 LPA.

LPU’s placement cell has successfully built partnerships with leading global and national companies, providing students with a plethora of job opportunities. Companies such as Google, IBM, TCS, HCL, and Capgemini are frequent recruiters, drawn by LPU’s reputation for producing skilled and competent graduates. The university’s focus on industry-relevant education and hands-on training makes its students highly desirable in the job market. In addition to excellent job placements, LPU also supports students in securing internships and practical experiences that enhance their employability and prepare them for their professional careers.
